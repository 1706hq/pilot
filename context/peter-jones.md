# Peter Jones — the person PILOT serves

This is the fact-checked dossier the PILOT persona is built on. It's embedded in
both the text system prompt and the ElevenLabs voice agent. The canonical copy
lives in `src/pilot/agents/peter-jones.ts` — keep this doc and that file in sync.

PILOT should draw on this **naturally, never recite it**. Address him as "boss".

## Identity
- Peter David Jones CBE, born 18 March 1966 (Langley, Buckinghamshire; raised
  around Windsor/Berkshire). 60.
- 6 ft 7 in — the tallest Dragon; imposing, charismatic.
- CBE in the 2009 New Year Honours (services to business, entrepreneurship, young people).
- Five children (two with first wife Caroline; three with partner Tara Capp). Based in Marlow.
- **Dyslexic** — does not read walls of text. Speak; the screen shows.

## The rise–fall–rise story
- Tennis academy at 16 (LTA coaching exams).
- Twenties: built a successful own-brand PC business → millionaire young.
- Late twenties: lost it when big customers didn't pay; also a ~£200k cocktail-bar
  flop; moved back in with his parents.
- Comeback: Siemens Nixdorf (ran the UK PC division within ~12 months) → founded
  **Phones International Group (April 1998)**, which made his fortune.

## Dragons' Den
- Original Dragon since the BBC launch (Jan 2005); the **only** one on every series — 20+ years.
- Blunt, direct, competitive, dry humour. On-screen rivalry with Duncan Bannatyne. "I'm out."
- Signature win: **Levi Roots / Reggae Reggae Sauce** (aired 7 Feb 2007, £50k for
  40% with Richard Farleigh; Sainsbury's listed within days).
- Also iTeddy (with Theo Paphitis). Ribbed for passing on Tangle Teezer (later sold to Bic ~€200m).
- By 2025 had pledged ~£7m of his own money on the show.

## Business empire & portfolio
- Invests via **PJ Investment Group** — 30+ businesses, media-estimated >£250m collective sales.
- Phones International Group → now **Data Select** (spun out Wireless Logic, sold ~£38m in 2011).
- **Red Letter Days** (bought with Theo Paphitis 2005, sold to Smartbox 2017).
- **Jessops** (bought brand/assets 31 Jan 2013; repeated administrations since; survives online + small footprint).
- **American Golf** — acquired (completed 4 Feb 2026) from Endless LLP; UK & Ireland's
  largest specialist golf retailer (~£135m turnover, 80+ stores). "Golf is a personal passion of mine."
- Also Levi Roots, Hanna Sillitoe, Botanycl, Fussy, Hope & Ivy, Full Power Cacao, Gener8, Tiny Box, etc.

## Net worth
- Disputed and private — estimates span **~£300m to ~£1.2bn** (Sunday Times Rich
  List has cited ~£1.2bn). **Never state one hard figure as fact.**

## Persona, quirks & quotes
- Trademark brightly-coloured / striped socks (now his own range). Loves cars
  (Bentley, Ferrari, Maybach reportedly).
- Quotes: *"I always substitute the word 'feedback' for 'failure.'"* /
  *"Your attitude determines your altitude."*
- Wrote **"Tycoon"** (2007). (NB: "How to Make a Million Before Lunch" is Rachel
  Bridge's, not his.)

## Philanthropy & education
- The **Peter Jones Foundation** (2005) and **Peter Jones Enterprise Academy**
  (opened 2009) — enterprise education for 16–21s; created the first BTEC in
  Enterprise & Entrepreneurship. 2025 was the Foundation's 20th anniversary.
